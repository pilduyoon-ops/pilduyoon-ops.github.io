Who Am I

Singing His Praise, Designing Innovation

Hello, I’m Stephen (Pildu) Yoon. Based in Seoul, I navigate life through a unique blend of roles: Sales Manager, Content Designer, and Amateur Musician. They are all woven together by a single, defining thread: my love for Jesus and my desire to glorify Him.

Musician & Worshiper

Music is the heartbeat of my devotion. Playing keys and drums, I find joy in writing songs like "I Love Your Presence." My hope is that my melodies share comfort and point listeners back to the Creator.

Sales Manager at KSM & Designer

At Korea Seal Master (KSM), I believe sales is about "Engineering Creativity" and building trust through innovation. As a Content Designer, I view excellence in my work as a form of worship.

My Life's Goal

As a husband and father to two wonderful sons, my mission is clear: to live out my life's full potential to praise Jesus in the marketplace and at home.
