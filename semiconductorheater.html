Welcome to Semiconductor Heater Design

Game Objective:

Design an optimal heater configuration to achieve precise temperature control on a semiconductor wafer. You must meet both the target average temperature AND maintain excellent temperature uniformity across the surface.

How to Play:
  • Click on green heater cells to toggle heaters on/off (inner = red, outer = orange)
  • Click on blue cooling cells to toggle cooling lines on/off
  • Adjust power sliders to control inner/outer heater power and cooling strength
  • Tune geometry parameters like material thickness, heater gap, and boundary zones
  • Select materials with different thermal properties (spreading vs efficiency)
Win Conditions:
  • Average temperature must be within the target range (Goal Temp ¬± tolerance)
  • Temperature uniformity (ŒîT = max - min) must be below the spec limit
  • Complete all 3 stages with increasing difficulty!
